How it works

01

Giveaways

Ones a week till mint a giveaway will take place in which will be 2 free mint (0.35 SOL) and 20 - 40 WL spots. It will be announced on twitter and discord.

NOTE: There will be WL spots for active members in the community (Twitter, Discord).

02

MINT

There will be two mints, pre-sale (0.2 SOL) and public sale (0.35 SOL). After the countdown, there will be a button called mint which will direct you to the mint site. (One week before the mint countdown will start)

NOTE: Date - TBA ( Late April )

The Money from the mint; 70% - liquidity pool (Tokenomics)        30% - development

03

staking

Right after mint starts the third phase and that’s staking which will be on Moon Bros official website. 
There will be connect button where holders can connect their wallets and start staking their Bro. 
From where will the money come from given to stakers? There will be royalties of 6% on every secondary market sale from first-generation and 4% from the second generation. All the money will be converted into MoonBros token and hand in hand given to stakers. 
There will be no rarity system on the first generation so every NFT will get the same amount.

NOTE: There will be no reward for non-stakers.

04

token

It will be a token on the Solana network, so any Solana wallet will do the magic. The first use case will take place at the staking and after that for the second generation.

05

Whitelist (Wl) for sec-gen

Whitelist ( WL ) for second-generation:Why whitelist when I just got a Bro? For sure with all the benefits MoonBros first-generation is already full Moon, but it wouldn’t be Bros without a surprise. Second generation MoonBros army is just around the corner and it needs their first-generation support. Second-generation will accept only whitelist spots from first-generation. 

NOTE: Only the first owners of first-generation will be whitelisted.

06

Second generation

DEX Exchange will take place after the mint second generation, and then we will start partnerships with other companies to launch their NFT collection or token on our platform.

Community will decide what comes next (trading exchange, crypto casino, marketplace, metaverse, gaming, and more..). Stay tuned on Twitter and Discord to decide.

What is NFT and why buy one?

An NFT — short for non-fungible token — essentially allows its buyer to say that they own the original copy of a digital file, in the same way you might own the original copy of a piece of physical art or the master file of a music recording.

Some NFTs have a utility like (Sports tickets, Gaming/Metaverse, Fashion/Luxury Goods, Identity, or like ours with passive income connected to an exchange) which makes them even hotter.
