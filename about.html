Frequently Asked Questions

Which network will Moon Bros be launched on?

Moon Bros will be launched on the Solana Blockchain network.

How much will the mint be?

For WL 0.2 Sol.
For Public 0.3 Sol.

How many Moon Bros will
I be able to mint?

You will be able to mint 1 per WL. Public sales won't have a limit per wallet.
30% ( 333 Moon Bros) for WL.
70% ( 778 Moon Bros) for the public.

Will there be a presale?
How can I get to the presale?

Yes, it will be available for a presale.
Presale will be available some hours before the public sale.
You can get the presale by engaging in the community, invites, giveaways...

What is the total supply of Moon Bros?

The total collection for Moon Bros will be 1111 unique NFT pieces.

What type of wallet can I use?

You will be able to use the Phantom, Solflare, Slope and Sollet wallets.

Is there a Rarity System?

Yes, it will be announced after sold out.


Which secondary marketplaces
will the collection be on?

We will be listed on Magic Eden, Solanart and Solsea.
